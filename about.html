Hi, I'm Anna Riedle

UX Designer

While my career path has not been straightforward, it has always had one commonality: developing and nurturing relationships with others and striving to meet their needs.

 

ABOUT ME

I aim to greet each day with compassion and enthusiasm, and my self-motivated and curious nature encourages me to learn something new every day and never give up when things get tough. I am an intuitive and ambitious UX Designer with a human-centered approach to research and design. I thrive in exciting environments and enjoy collaborating as a team player.

 

Manifesting UX started as a mantra, a personal goal to work hard and learn everything I could until I became a UX designer, but as I began examining the user's motivations, emotions, and experiences, it grew into something more - I realized that the designs created in the process were themselves...a manifestation of the user's experience.

 

FUN FACT

I have a BS in Research and Experimental Psychology from the University of Central Florida. I am a member of the Omicron Delta Kappa chapter of the National Honors Society of Leadership and Success, and a member of the Kappa Nu Chapter of the National Honors Society.
